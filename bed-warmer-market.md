<p><p>The bed warmer market is divided into two main types: underblankets and overblankets. Underblankets are placed directly on the mattress, providing consistent warmth by heating the bed from underneath, making them ideal for colder climates. Overblankets, on the other hand, are layered on top of existing bedding and can be used as additional warmth or during cold nights. Both types cater to comfort needs, with variations in materials and features enhancing the user's sleeping experience.</p></p>
