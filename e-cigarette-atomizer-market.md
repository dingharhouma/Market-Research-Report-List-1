<p><p>The E-cigarette atomizer market includes Rebuildable Drip Atomizers (RDA) and Rebuildable Tank Atomizers (RTA). RDAs are designed for direct dripping of e-liquid onto coils, allowing for enhanced flavor and vapor production, but require frequent dripping. In contrast, RTAs feature a tank that holds e-liquid, providing a more convenient sustained vaping experience with reduced need for frequent refills. Both types appeal to advanced users seeking customization and performance, driving growth in the e-cigarette sector.</p></p>
