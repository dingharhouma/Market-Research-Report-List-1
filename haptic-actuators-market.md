<p><p>The haptic actuators market comprises several types, including Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs), and others. ERM actuators use a rotating unbalanced mass to create vibrations, commonly found in mobile devices. LRAs, on the other hand, generate precise vibrations through a vibrating element aligned with the linear motion, ideal for wearables and gaming devices. Other actuators may include piezoelectric and pneumatic types, catering to specialized applications in robotics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
