<p><p>The lithium cobaltate market is primarily categorized into two types: spherical and layered. Spherical lithium cobaltate features a rounded morphology, improving electrode performance and energy density, making it suitable for high-capacity lithium-ion batteries. In contrast, layered lithium cobaltate has a structured, stacked arrangement of layers, enhancing conductivity and stability, which is beneficial for long-cycle applications. Both types cater to different requirements in electronics and electric vehicles, with preferences based on performance characteristics and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
