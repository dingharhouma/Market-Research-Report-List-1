<p><strong>The Microplate Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate readers market is primarily segmented into two types: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter microplate readers utilize specific filters to isolate wavelengths, making them ideal for fluorescence and absorbance measurements. They offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, optical grating microplate readers employ a diffraction grating to disperse light across a spectrum, allowing for greater flexibility in wavelength selection and enabling advanced applications like luminescence and time-resolved fluorescence assays, providing higher sensitivity and precision.</p></p>
