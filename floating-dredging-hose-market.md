<p><p>The floating dredging hose market comprises two main types: flexible and hard (non-flexible) hoses. Flexible hoses are designed to bend and accommodate dynamic movements, allowing for ease of handling and installation in various marine environments. They are typically used for suction and discharge operations where mobility is essential. Hard hoses, on the other hand, are rigid and maintain a fixed shape, providing durability and strength for high-pressure applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
