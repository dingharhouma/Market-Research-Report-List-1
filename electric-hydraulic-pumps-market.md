<p><strong>The Electric Hydraulic Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston</li><li>Positive-Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric hydraulic pumps market features key types such as piston and positive-displacement pumps. Piston pumps utilize movable pistons to create hydraulic pressure, offering high efficiency and output, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, positive-displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of hydraulic fluid and forcing it into the discharge line, ensuring a consistent flow regardless of resistance. Both types cater to various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and automotive, driven by the need for precise hydraulic control and increased efficiency.</p></p>
