<p><p>The ceiling heaters market is primarily divided into two types: panel heaters and cassette heaters. Panel heaters are sleek, flat units installed directly onto ceilings, providing efficient warmth and promoting even heat distribution in a room. Cassette heaters, on the other hand, are integrated into the ceiling's false structure, offering a discreet heating solution while utilizing a high-performance design to optimize space and aesthetics. Both types are popular for their energy efficiency and space-saving capabilities in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
