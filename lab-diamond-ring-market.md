<p><p>The lab diamond ring market primarily consists of two types of diamonds: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and HTHP (High Pressure High Temperature). CVD diamonds are created by simulating natural diamond growth in a controlled environment, allowing for high purity and diverse color options. HTHP diamonds, on the other hand, are formed using extreme pressure and temperature, replicating the natural diamond formation process. Both types offer an ethical and environmentally friendly alternative to mined diamonds, appealing to a growing market of conscious consumers.</p></p>
