<p><p>The fluorine-containing polymer market is primarily divided into two main types: perfluoropolymer and partially fluorinated polymer. Perfluoropolymers are fully fluorinated, offering superior chemical resistance, thermal stability, and low friction, making them ideal for applications in electronics, chemicals, and aerospace. Partially fluorinated polymers, while having some fluorine content, provide a balance between performance and cost, often used in coatings, seals, and gaskets. Together, these types cater to diverse industry needs, emphasizing durability and chemical resilience.</p></p>
