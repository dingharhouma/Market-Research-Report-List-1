<p><p>The ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil) explosives market includes three main types: Powdered ANFO Explosive, Porous Granular ANFO Explosive, and Heavy ANFO Explosive. Powdered ANFO is finely ground, offering high energy output and ease of mixing. Porous Granular ANFO features porous granules for better oil absorption, enhancing stability and performance. Heavy ANFO contains added materials like aluminum or other sensitizers, increasing density and blast power for tougher applications. Each type caters to specific mining, quarrying, and construction needs, balancing cost, efficiency, and safety.</p></p>
