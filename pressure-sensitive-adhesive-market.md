PSAs are commonly found in tapes, labels, stickers, and films.</p><p>The Pressure-sensitive Adhesive Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand from end-use industries and advancements in adhesive technologies. <p><p>The Pressure-sensitive Adhesive (PSA) market includes various types based on formulation and application. **Water-based PSAs** use water as a solvent, offering eco-friendliness and low VOC emissions, ideal for labels and tapes. **Solvent-based PSAs** provide strong adhesion and durability, suitable for harsh environments. **Hot Melt PSAs** are thermoplastic adhesives applied in molten form, offering fast bonding and versatility. **Radiation-cured PSAs** use UV or electron beams for curing, ensuring high performance in electronics and medical applications. **Other markets** include hybrid or specialty adhesives tailored for niche uses.</p></p>
