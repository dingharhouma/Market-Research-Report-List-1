<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Plastic (FRP) pipes are categorized into three main types: Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP) pipes, Glass Reinforced Epoxy (GRE) pipes, and Glass Reinforced Vinylester (GRV) pipes. GRP pipes are known for their corrosion resistance and lightweight properties, making them ideal for water and wastewater applications. GRE pipes offer enhanced mechanical strength and thermal resistance, suitable for industrial settings. GRV pipes combine the benefits of GRP with improved chemical resistance, catering to specialized applications in harsh environments.</p></p>
