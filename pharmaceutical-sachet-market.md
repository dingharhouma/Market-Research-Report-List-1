<p><p>The pharmaceutical sachet market consists of single dose and double dose sachets, designed for the convenient delivery of medications. Single dose sachets contain a pre-measured amount of medication for immediate use, ensuring precise dosing and enhanced portability. In contrast, double dose sachets provide two servings of the drug, catering to patients requiring more than one dose at a time, which helps reduce packaging waste and simplifies medication management. Both formats enhance patient adherence and support efficient distribution in healthcare settings.</p></p>
