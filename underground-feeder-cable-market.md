<p><strong>The Underground Feeder Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Twisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underground Feeder (UF) cable market can be categorized into two primary types: solid and twisted pairs. Solid UF cables consist of a single conductor, offering low resistance and durability, making them ideal for short-distance power distribution. In contrast, twisted pair UF cables feature multiple insulated wires twisted together, which helps reduce electromagnetic interference and increases efficiency over longer distances. Both types are essential for reliable underground power delivery, with applications ranging from residential to commercial setups.</p></p>
